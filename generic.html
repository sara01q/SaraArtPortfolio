About me

  My name is Diaconu Sara and I'm studying Industrial Design at Brasov, Romania. I am a passionate and skilled illustrator that can create visually appealing artworks. My creations are characterised by vibrant color palettes, the unique characters and eye catching art. The programs I'm using for illustrations, UI design and personal work are Procreate, Photoshop, Paint Tool Sai, InDesign, Illustrator, Clip Studio Paint. For 3D modeling I'm using 3DsMax, Maya, Catia, Blender.
